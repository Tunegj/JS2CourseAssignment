## OVERVIEW

I use AI (ChatGPT, Google) consistently during my learning process. I ask it questions about concepts I don't understand, and it provides explanations and examples. This helps me grasp new topics quicker.

During development AI was used as a learning and debugging support tool. All the suggestions by AI were reviewed, tested and understood before being implemented.
